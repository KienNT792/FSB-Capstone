Review a thesis section draft for academic quality.

Severity levels: [Critical], [Major], [Minor]

Check for:
- Unsupported claims (missing citation)
- Vague research contribution (not measurable)
- Mismatch between claim and experiment design
- Informal register or hedging language
- Missing limitations discussion
- Figure/table not referenced in text

Output format:
- Severity-ranked issue list
- Concrete rewrite for every [Critical] and [Major] issue
- Overall section verdict: Ready / Needs revision / Major rework
